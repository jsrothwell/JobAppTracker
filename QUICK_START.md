## Common Questions

**Q: Where is my data saved?**
A: Locally in UserDefaults on your Mac. No cloud sync.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Not yet - future feature!

**Q: Why is Analytics empty?**
A: Add at least 1 job to see metrics. Add 5+ jobs for meaningful charts.

**Q: How do I change a job's status?**
A: Either drag it in Kanban view, or edit the job in List view.

**Q: Do reminders send notifications?**
A: Not yet - they show as badges for now.
